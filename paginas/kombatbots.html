kombat bots

Allow me to introduce you to our thrilling project: "Kombat Bots." These two homemade combat robots draw their inspiration from the iconic characters in the popular game Mortal Kombat, Scorpion and Sub-Zero. These robots represent a fusion of engineering prowess and creative ingenuity.

At the heart of each Kombat Bot are two powerful motors, meticulously attached to the back wheels, providing the driving force for these formidable machines. To enhance their maneuverability and agility, we've incorporated a freewheel mechanism in the front. This allows our robots to swiftly navigate the arena, just like their video game counterparts. It's worth noting that both robots were completely crafted with a 3D printer and were designed using FreeCAD, showcasing the cutting-edge technology and innovation behind their construction. For a closer look at the intricate 3D design of these robots, you can view detailed images

What truly sets these robots apart are their unique weaponry systems, inspired by the signature weapons of Scorpion and Sub-Zero. Each robot wields its own distinct weapon, meticulously designed and crafted to pay homage to the characters' legendary abilities. Whether it's Scorpion's fearsome spear or Sub-Zero's icy weaponry, these bots bring the essence of Mortal Kombat into the physical realm.

But here's where the excitement truly begins. To control these combat machines, we've developed a specialized mobile application. This app is your gateway to commanding Scorpion and Sub-Zero with precision and finesse. You can install this app on your smartphone and connect to the bots via Bluetooth. Once connected, you'll have full control over their movements and weaponry, just like a true kombatant!

Our journey in creating these Kombat Bots has been a testament to the fusion of technology, creativity, and passion. From the initial concept sketches to the intricate engineering designs, every step of the way has been a thrilling adventure. We've painstakingly documented our progress, ensuring that fellow enthusiasts can follow along and be part of our journey.

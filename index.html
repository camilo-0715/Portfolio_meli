Who am I?

UX/UI Designer based in Berlin, Germany. I'm María Melissa Rodrigo López, a Digital Marketing and UX/UI Specialist with a Master's in Multidevice Web Design and a background in Marketing and Advertising. Creative and detail-oriented, I combine technical expertise in interface design and usability with hands-on experience in social media management, branding, and digital communication.

My expertise spans UX Research & Methods including user personas, competitive analysis, user flows, and information architecture, alongside UI Design skills in wireframing, advanced prototyping, responsive interfaces, and design systems. I also bring valuable Digital Marketing experience in social media strategy, branding, content creation, and campaign management.

I'm passionate about delivering innovative, user-centered digital experiences through thoughtful design and strategic thinking. My multilingual abilities (native Spanish, full working proficiency in English) and diverse professional background allow me to approach projects from multiple perspectives, always keeping the end user at the center of the design process.
